David Burgess: Freelance Digital Art Director / Digital Designer / Interactive Designer
Over 20 years advertising agency experience, 15 plus specialising in digital, producing projects from concept to completion for many blue chip clients, working on global briefs. I have worked on many multi-channel projects / pitches creating off and online advertising campaigns, bespoke websites driven by content management systems (CMSs), motion graphics, microsites, video, mobile and tablet applications (Apps).

Skills: Ideas generation, conceptual and strategic thinking, digital art direction, typography, UI, UX design, interface design, conceptual interactive design, interactive marketing, online advertising, video, motion graphics and a thorough understanding of how these elements work together for an end solution.

Previous role: Senior Digital Art Director at McCann-Erickson Manchester Pre May 2009 I was a senior member of the McCann-Erickson digital team for over ten years, overseeing and mentoring numerous members of staff, all with varied skill sets and goals. Please get in touch with any questions or interesting projects.

If you would like to work with me or require anymore information please get in-touch, call 07980 299 127 or email david@pixelsoup.co.uk
